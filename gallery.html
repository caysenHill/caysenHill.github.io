Did You Know?

The reason some Whisky is spelled "whiskey" instead of "whisky" is actually because the Irish wanted a way to descern their products from their Scotish neighbors, so they added an "e" into the name! Because of that, most distilleries with that produce "Whiskey" actually have roots, in one way or another, connected to the Irish!
